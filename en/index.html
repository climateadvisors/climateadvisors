About Us

Climate Advisors is a Brazilian consultancy specializing in transforming complex analyses into high-impact **advocacy and international negotiation strategies**. Founded by a senior professional with 13 years of experience, we operate at the intersection of **climate change, international trade, and sustainability**.

We possess extensive knowledge of the Brazilian federal Executive and Legislative branches and broad experience in designing and executing political advocacy campaigns. Our focus includes industrial climate diplomacy, climate finance, and mapping and addressing sustainability trade barriers.
